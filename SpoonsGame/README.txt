Compile command: javac Card.java Dealer.java Deck.java Game.java Player.java SpoonPile.java SpoonsDriver.java
Rules of game:
- played with 4 players
- one less spoon than players
- cards dealt randomly
- each player starts with 4 cards 
- goal is to gather a set of 4 cards with the same rank, and then discreetly take a spoon
- player left without a spoon loses
